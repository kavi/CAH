A Game is played over a number of rounds. At the end of each round the player(s) who won the round receives 1 point.

A round is divided into 3 phases

  • First every player plays an answer (white card) from his hand that completes the black card for this round
  • Then the played cards are revealed and you vote for the one you find the most funny, awkward, revolting or just plain silly - you decide (except you can never vote on yourself..)
  • The round ends with of summary of the votes
